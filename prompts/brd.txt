# Business Requirements Document

## 1. Executive Summary
- Project overview and background
- Business objectives and goals
- Scope and deliverables
- Success criteria and benefits
- Budget and timeline overview

## 2. Business Context
- Current state analysis
- Business problem or opportunity
- Market analysis and competitive landscape
- Regulatory and compliance requirements
- Stakeholder analysis

## 3. Business Objectives
- Primary business objectives
- Secondary objectives
- Success metrics and KPIs
- Return on investment (ROI) expectations
- Risk mitigation goals

## 4. Functional Requirements
### 4.1 Core Business Functions
- Primary business processes
- User roles and responsibilities
- Business rules and workflows
- Data requirements

### 4.2 User Requirements
- User personas and profiles
- User stories and scenarios
- User experience requirements
- Accessibility requirements

### 4.3 System Requirements
- Functional specifications
- Performance requirements
- Integration requirements
- Reporting and analytics needs

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- Performance and scalability requirements
- Security and privacy requirements
- Reliability and availability requirements
- Usability and user experience requirements
- Compliance and regulatory requirements

## 6. Business Rules and Constraints
- Business logic and validation rules
- Data governance and quality rules
- Approval workflows and processes
- Audit and compliance rules

## 7. Assumptions and Dependencies
- Project assumptions
- External dependencies
- Resource dependencies
- Technology dependencies

## 8. Acceptance Criteria
- Feature acceptance criteria
- Performance acceptance criteria
- Quality acceptance criteria
- User acceptance testing requirements

## 9. Risk Analysis
- Business risks and mitigation strategies
- Technical risks and contingencies
- Project risks and management plans
- Change management considerations

## 10. Implementation Approach
- Phased implementation strategy
- Change management plan
- Training and support requirements
- Communication plan

Use professional business language and ensure all requirements are specific, measurable, and testable. Include clear acceptance criteria for each requirement.
